Part Two.

Code and design your own front-end website! If you want to make yourself a personal page with a bit about yourself, what you've done with your life, your resume, etc, that's an option. If you want to do something else, you can re-design the course website. We'll be replacing the current course website with the TAs' favourite of all re-designs.
